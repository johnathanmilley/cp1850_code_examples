## Boolean expressions

- Relational operators

    ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=

    - careful with floating point numbers (rounding issue)

    **Examples:**
    - integers
    - int(age) = 28
    - strings

- Logical operators

    - and (both expressions are True)
    - or  (one of two expressions is True)
    - not (reverse the boolean value)

    **Examples:**
    - 1
    - 2
    - 3

- Comparing Strings
    - new methods: `upper()` / `lower()`
    - **Format:** *string*.upper()

    **Examples:**
    - 1
    - 2
    - 3
